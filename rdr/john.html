John Marston

Height: 6 feet 0 inch.
Weight: 180lbs.
Gang: Van Der Linde.

John Marston is a central character in *Red Dead Redemption 2* and the protagonist of the original *Red Dead Redemption*. Once a key member of the Van der Linde gang, John is a skilled gunslinger and a man of few words. In *RDR2*, he struggles with his role in the gang, his strained relationship with his partner Abigail, and his responsibilities as a father to his son, Jack. As the story unfolds, John begins to distance himself from the gang's chaos, yearning for a stable and honest life for his family. Guided and supported by Arthur Morgan, John eventually seeks redemption by leaving behind his outlaw past. His journey is one of transformation, evolving from a reluctant criminal to a devoted family man. John's story is a powerful exploration of redemption, loyalty, and the fight to build a better future.
