Arthur Morgan

Height: 5 feet 10 inches.
Weight: 180lbs.
Gang: Van Der Linde.

Arthur Morgan is the central protagonist of *Red Dead Redemption 2* (RDR2), set in 1899 during the decline of the Wild West. He is a senior member of the Van der Linde gang, led by Dutch Van der Linde, and serves as a loyal enforcer. Arthur is a complex character, embodying both the ruthless traits of an outlaw and a deep capacity for reflection and morality.
